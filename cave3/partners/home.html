Spanda Foundation ~  A The Hague (NL) based independent, non-profit International Civil Society Organisation (ICSO) for Development to catalyse sustainable long-term systemic change and promote creative solutions for the attainment of a higher individual and collective state of consciousness. The Foundation is in Consultative Status with the UN Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC).

A New York-based innovative global multi-disciplinary artist boutique management and producing agency, specialising in special projects and offering representation and career development services.
